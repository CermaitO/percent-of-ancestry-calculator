Percent of Ancestry Calculator

How much of your ancestry does a particular ancestor account for? Enter your ancestors relationship with you, such as great grandparent or great x 13 grandparent etc, and the amount of your ancestry from this ancestor will be calculated

Note that this does NOT calculate how much of your DNA is from this person. Only a DNA test could tell you that. This simply measures how much of your ancestry, not DNA, comes from this person.
